About

A trusted intermediary, by design.

Mineora is the operating brand of 1000175534 Ontario Ltd., a mine brokerage incorporated under Canadian law and operating across Mexico.

Most of the global mining transaction market operates informally — handshake agreements, unverified grades, and broker chains five layers deep. Mineora exists to offer something different: a single, accountable, Canadian counterparty between qualified international buyers and verified Mexican mine owners.

Founder

Mineora was founded by Loana Vanessa Danjoux, who manages owner relationships and transaction structure. The firm holds exclusive or semi-exclusive broker agreements with each property in the active portfolio.

What we do

We broker direct acquisitions of certified mining properties. We do not list rumours, optionality plays, or unverified concessions. Every property in the portfolio has a signed broker agreement, a documented concession title, and lab-certified assay data on file before it is ever shown to a prospective buyer.

What we don't do

We are not a fund, a syndicate, or a finder. We do not represent buyers — we represent owners, and we are paid only at closing. Our incentive is simple: complete real, well-documented transactions on terms both sides can stand behind.

Corporate

1000175534 Ontario Ltd. is incorporated in the Province of Ontario, Canada. All transactions are governed by Ontario law and supported by Canadian counsel.
