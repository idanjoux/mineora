Mark Concept

A geometric arc inscribed in a precision circle — suggesting both movement and circumference (the shape of the earth, the arc of a deal closing). The center dot anchors focus. No gradients, no effects: the mark works at 16px as a favicon and at 400px on a business card. Playfair Display Medium carries the wordmark — old-money serif confidence without being corporate.

Implementation Guide

🌐 Wix Studio Setup

  • Use Wix Studio — not the basic editor
  • Start from the "Blank" template
  • Import Google Fonts: Playfair Display + Epilogue
  • Set page max-width to 1080px, centered
  • Connect your domain in Domain Settings
  • Set body background to #FFFFFF, text to #0A0A0A

📐 Page Structure (v1)

  • Home — hero, stats, portfolio table, process, CTA
  • Portfolio — all 10 mines, filterable by mineral
  • How It Works — process + FAQ
  • About — Loana's bio + company credentials
  • Contact / NDA — DocuSign embed or Wix form
  • Privacy + Terms — legal compliance

✦ Design Rules

  • No accent colors — grayscale only
  • Playfair Display for all headings, no exceptions
  • Buttons: solid black, 4px radius, no gradients
  • Borders: 1px solid #E8E8E8 only
  • Min 80px top/bottom padding on every section
  • Data in Epilogue 500 — grades, prices, hectares
