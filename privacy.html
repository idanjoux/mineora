Privacy policy.

1000175534 Ontario Ltd. (operating as Mineora) collects only the personal information necessary to evaluate prospective buyers and execute mine brokerage transactions.

Information we collect

Contact details (name, email, phone, organization), professional background relevant to qualification as a buyer, and any information voluntarily submitted via our contact form or NDA process.

How we use it

To respond to inquiries, qualify prospective buyers, execute non-disclosure agreements, and facilitate transactions. We do not sell, rent, or share contact information with any third party other than the mine owner whose property is being introduced, and only after a signed NDA is in place.

Data retention

We retain inquiry and transaction records for the period required by Canadian commercial law and our broker obligations.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be directed via the contact form.
